## Common Introduction Questions

1. "Tell me about yourself"
2. "Walk me through your resume"
3. "Why do you want to work here?"
4. "What do you know about our company?"
5. "How did you hear about this position?"

## Behavioral Questions (STAR Method)

### Leadership & Teamwork
1. "Tell me about a time you led a team through a difficult project"
2. "Describe a situation where you had to work with a difficult colleague"
3. "Give an example of how you handled a team conflict"
4. "Share a time when you had to motivate your team"

### Problem Solving
1. "Describe a complex problem you solved"
2. "Tell me about a time you identified and fixed a process issue"
3. "Share an example of when you had to make a quick decision"
4. "Describe a situation where you had limited information but needed to act"

### Adaptability & Learning
1. "Tell me about a time you had to adapt to a major change"
2. "Describe how you learned a new skill quickly"
3. "Share an example of when you had to pivot your approach"
4. "Tell me about a failure and what you learned from it"

## Technical Questions by Role

### Software Engineer
1. "Explain the difference between == and === in JavaScript"
2. "What is the time complexity of binary search?"
3. "How would you design a URL shortener?"
4. "Explain REST vs GraphQL"

### Product Manager
1. "How would you prioritize this feature list?"
2. "Tell me about a product you launched from ideation to launch"
3. "How do you gather user feedback?"
4. "How would you measure success for this product?"

### Data Scientist
1. "Explain bias-variance tradeoff"
2. "How would you handle imbalanced dataset?"
3. "Describe your experience with A/B testing"
4. "How would you explain a complex model to stakeholders?"

### UX/UI Designer
1. "Walk me through your design process"
2. "How do you approach user research?"
3. "Describe a design challenge you overcame"
4. "How do you balance user needs vs. business goals?"

## Company-Specific Patterns

### FAANG (Facebook, Amazon, Apple, Netflix, Google)
- Emphasis on scalability and large-scale systems
- Behavioral questions about leadership principles
- Technical depth and breadth
- Innovation and "moonshot" thinking

### Startups
- Adaptability and wearing multiple hats
- Resourcefulness with limited resources
- Entrepreneurial mindset
- Fast-paced decision making

### Traditional Companies
- Domain knowledge and industry experience
- Process and structure orientation
- Risk mitigation and stability focus
- Long-term career goals alignment

## STAR Method Guide

**Situation**: Set the context
- What was the situation?
- When did it happen?
- Who was involved?

**Task**: What needed to be done
- What was your responsibility?
- What were the challenges?

**Action**: What did you do
- What specific steps did you take?
- What was your contribution?

**Result**: What happened
- What was the outcome?
- What did you learn?
- Any metrics or achievements?

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Being too generic**: Add specific details and examples
2. **Talking too long**: Keep answers concise (2-3 minutes)
3. **Being negative**: Never speak poorly of past employers
4. **Not asking questions**: Show genuine interest
5. **Over-prepared answers**: Sound natural, not rehearsed

---

## Tips for Remote Interviews

1. **Test your technology**: Camera, mic, internet connection
2. **Professional setting**: Clean background, good lighting
3. **Minimize distractions**: Close other apps, notifications
4. **Look at the camera**: Not just the screen
5. **Have notes ready**: But don't read from them

## Questions to Ask the Interviewer

1. "What does success look like in this role?"
2. "How would you describe the team culture?"
3. "What are the biggest challenges the team is facing?"
4. "What opportunities for growth and development exist?"
5. "How does the company approach innovation?"

---

## Follow-Up Questions After Interview

1. **Timeline**: "When can I expect to hear back?"
2. **Next Steps**: "What are the next stages in the process?"
3. **Contact**: "Who can I reach out to if I have questions?"
4. **Thank You**: Send a personalized thank-you note within 24 hours
